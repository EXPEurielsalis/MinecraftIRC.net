In order to reinstall Minecraft, follow these instructions based on your OS.
This will delete all Minecraft files (including resource packs, server lists, settings, screenshots, logs, etc.) except for your worlds. Your Realms worlds and Mojang account will also not be affected.


Windows
  1. Press your Windows and R keys, to bring up the "Run" dialog
  2. In the prompt, type in, %AppData%\.minecraft
  3. Delete everything except the saves folder
Mac
  1. Open a Terminal window. The Terminal application can be found under Finder -> Go -> Applications -> Utilities.
  2. Copy and paste this into the terminal (right-click -> Paste): curl -L {{ site.url }}/static/scripts/reinstall-minecraft.sh | sh
Linux
  1. Open a Terminal window.
  2. Copy and paste this into the terminal curl -L {{ site.url }}/static/scripts/reinstall-minecraft.sh | sh

Once you restart your client, the game will automatically reinstall the necessary files.
